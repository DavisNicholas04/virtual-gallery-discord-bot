Introduction

The theme for our art gallery will be based on space in various media. These media will include things such as anime, video games, and the history of space travel and exploration in our world. We will use this gallery to show the differences and similarities between video games and anime versus our real-life history on the topic of space exploration and space in general.You will view this virtual gallery in our discord bot.

History

Humans have always looked up into the night sky and dreamed about space. In the latter half of the 20th century, rockets were developed that were powerful enough to overcome the force of gravity to reach orbital velocities, paving the way for space exploration to become a reality.
